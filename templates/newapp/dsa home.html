Longing to travel? Pack your bags, time to leave!

Our website here will help you plan your trip with a short and economic travel experience.



Our project, titled Smart Travel Planner(STP) is a user friendly interface that helps you plan your trip to anywhere in the world! It will help you find the fastest travel path between all the places you want to visit and it will give you an economic travel as well. We've always found it tedious to look up places and collect information about the place we want to visit, and many a times feel lazy to do so because there's a lot of searching involved. Even if we decide upon the place and the tourist spots in that place, the trip is not often economical and travel consumes a lot of time and energy. This was our inspiration to take up this project.

INFORMATION:

To access all the features of the website, you have to sign-up first! Upon logging onto the website, you can search for a place to view information about it: like the tourist spots or the best airbnb deals in that place. To plan your trip, you have to choose the places you want to visit. Once chosen, leave it to us to optimise a plan just for you, at the lowest possible cost and the fastest possible travel route!!
We used a graph data structure to create the perfect plan:)
